Kinematics Calculator

Welcome to Kinematics Calculator.

This is a simple tool that allows you to calculate 1 variable with at least 3 parameters given.

The calculator will tell you the answer round to 5 decimal places, and which equation is used.

Both linear and rotational kinematics apply, only the units are different.

Toggling between linear and rotational simply changes the base unit between meter and radian.

Just a friendly reminder, you are not advised to use this calculator until you really understand and remember the functions.

This is just a tool for your convenience when doing practices at home, verifying answers, etc.

Remember, you do not have access to this calculator during the AP Exam.
